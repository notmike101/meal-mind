## Ingredients
- 2 large flour tortillas
- 6 oz sliced turkey
- 1 avocado
- 1 cup romaine lettuce
- 1 tomato
- 2 tbsp Greek yogurt

## Instructions
1. Spread Greek yogurt on each tortilla.
2. Add turkey, sliced avocado, lettuce, and tomato.
3. Roll tightly and slice in half.
