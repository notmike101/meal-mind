## Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1 cup white rice
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder

## Instructions
1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
2. Season chicken with garlic powder and cook in olive oil.
3. Steam broccoli until tender.
4. Serve chicken and broccoli over rice with soy sauce.
