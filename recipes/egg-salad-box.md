## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 2 tbsp Greek yogurt
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 2 celery stalks
- 4 slices whole grain bread
- 1 cup grapes

## Instructions
1. Boil eggs, cool, and chop.
2. Mix eggs with Greek yogurt, mustard, and diced celery.
3. Pack with bread and grapes.
