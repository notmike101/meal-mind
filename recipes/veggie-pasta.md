## Ingredients
- 8 oz penne pasta
- 1 zucchini
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 cup marinara sauce
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Boil pasta until al dente.
2. Saute zucchini and bell pepper in olive oil.
3. Toss pasta with vegetables and marinara.
4. Top with parmesan.
