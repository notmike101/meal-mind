## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets
- 1 cup jasmine rice
- 2 cups green beans
- 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dill

## Instructions
1. Cook jasmine rice.
2. Roast salmon with olive oil, lemon, and dill.
3. Steam green beans.
4. Serve salmon with rice and green beans.
