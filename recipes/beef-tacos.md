## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 6 corn tortillas
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 tbsp taco seasoning

## Instructions
1. Brown ground beef in a skillet.
2. Stir in taco seasoning and a splash of water.
3. Warm tortillas.
4. Fill tortillas with beef, lettuce, tomato, and cheddar.
