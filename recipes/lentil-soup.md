## Ingredients
- 1 cup dried lentils
- 1 onion
- 2 carrots
- 2 celery stalks
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Dice onion, carrots, and celery.
2. Saute vegetables in olive oil.
3. Add lentils, broth, and cumin.
4. Simmer until lentils are tender.
