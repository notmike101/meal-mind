## Ingredients
- 1 can chickpeas
- 1 cucumber
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1/2 cup feta cheese
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 2 tbsp Greek dressing

## Instructions
1. Rinse chickpeas.
2. Chop cucumber and tomatoes.
3. Combine chickpeas, vegetables, feta, quinoa, and dressing.
